# Delta Dental Programs and Services FAQ

## What is DeltaVision?
DeltaVision is a vision benefits add-on offered through Delta Dental's partnership with VSP Vision Care. It complements dental benefits with vision coverage — two programs in one convenient package. DeltaVision offers value-packed plans with savings on eye exams and glasses, plus extra benefits like discounts on LASIK and additional pairs of glasses. VSP has the largest vision network nationwide and consistently earns a 99% member satisfaction rating. Find an eye doctor at vsp.com/eye-doctor.

## What is the Special Health Care Needs Benefit?
Delta Dental offers enhanced benefits for members with intellectual or developmental disabilities. Dental care is the leading unmet health care need for millions of U.S. children and adults with a disability — approximately 6.5 million people in the U.S. The benefit includes:
- Additional pre-treatment visits to help patients learn what to expect
- Up to 4 dental cleanings per benefit year (vs. the standard 2)
- Treatment delivery modifications including anesthesia and nitrous oxide for patients with sensory sensitivities, behavioral challenges, severe anxiety, or other barriers to treatment
This is a standard benefit on most group and individual dental plans administered by Delta Dental of Michigan.

## What is the LifeSmile Wellness Program?
LifeSmile Wellness is Delta Dental's oral wellness program focused on the connection between oral health and overall body health. It includes oral health articles, teaching tools, recommended resources, and oral health rack cards. The program emphasizes that "Dentists Are Disease Detectives" — the mouth provides clues to other diseases. Dental coverage is linked to better overall health outcomes.

## What is myDentalScore?
myDentalScore is an online oral disease risk assessment tool. Members answer simple questions to assess their risk level for oral disease.

## What are Delta Dental's Medicare Advantage Partners in Michigan?
Delta Dental partners with several Michigan health plans to offer dental benefits through Medicare Advantage (Part C). Traditional Medicare does NOT include dental benefits, but Medicare Advantage often includes vision, hearing, and dental coverage. Partners include:
- HAP Medicare Advantage (hap.org/medicare)
- Priority Health Medicare Advantage (priorityhealth.com/medicare)
- CCA Health Michigan (commonwealthcarealliance.org/mi)
- PHP Medicare (phpmedicare.com)
- McLaren Medicare (mclarenhealthplan.org/medicare)

## What is Healthy Kids Dental?
Healthy Kids Dental is Delta Dental's pediatric dental program providing dental benefits for children. Pediatric plans have no waiting periods, and the out-of-pocket maximum is $375 per child or $750 per family.

## What is Passport Dental?
Passport Dental provides out-of-area coverage for Delta Dental members who are traveling or living outside their home state.

## How does Coordination of Benefits work?
Coordination of Benefits (COB) applies when a person is covered by more than one dental plan — for example, when both a husband and wife have employer dental coverage. The goal is to ensure the combined payment from both plans does not exceed the total claim amount. Delta Dental follows rules established by state law to determine which plan is the primary payer and which is secondary. When Delta Dental is primary, it pays the full benefit allowed by contract. When secondary, it pays up to what it would have paid as primary or the total claim amount.

## What is a Pre-treatment Estimate?
A pre-treatment estimate is a request members can submit before a procedure to get an estimate of what Delta Dental will pay and what the member will owe. This helps members plan for costs before treatment begins.
