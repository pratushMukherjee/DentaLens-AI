# Delta Dental PPO Silver Plan

## Plan Overview
- **Plan ID:** DD-PPO-SILVER-2024
- **Network:** Delta Dental PPO
- **Annual Maximum:** $1,500 per member
- **Deductible:** $75 individual / $225 family
- **Plan Year:** January 1 – December 31

## Coverage Summary

### Preventive & Diagnostic Services (100% coverage, no deductible)
- D0120 Periodic oral evaluation: Covered 2x per calendar year
- D0210 Full mouth X-rays: Covered 1x per 5 years
- D0274 Bitewing X-rays (4 films): Covered 1x per calendar year
- D1110 Adult prophylaxis (cleaning): Covered 2x per calendar year
- D1120 Child prophylaxis: Covered 2x per calendar year
- D1206 Fluoride varnish: Covered 1x per year (under age 14)

### Basic Services (70% after deductible)
- D2140-D2161 Amalgam fillings: Covered
- D2391-D2394 Resin composite fillings (posterior): Covered
- D3310-D3330 Root canal therapy: Covered
- D4341 Periodontal scaling and root planing: Covered, 1x per quadrant per 2 years
- D7140 Extraction, erupted tooth: Covered
- D7210 Surgical extraction: Covered

### Major Services (40% after deductible)
- D2740-D2750 Crowns: Covered, 1x per tooth per 7 years
- D5110-D5120 Complete dentures: Covered, 1x per 7 years
- D6240 Pontic: Covered
- D6750 Crown abutment: Covered

### Orthodontic Services (Not Covered)
- Orthodontic services are not included in the Silver plan
- Upgrade to the Gold plan for orthodontic coverage

## Waiting Periods
- Preventive & Diagnostic: None
- Basic Services: 6 months
- Major Services: 12 months

## Exclusions
- Cosmetic procedures
- Dental implants
- Orthodontic services
- Services covered under medical insurance
- Experimental treatments
- Procedures started before plan effective date

## In-Network vs Out-of-Network
- **In-network PPO dentists:** No balance billing
- **Out-of-network dentists:** Covered at reduced fee schedule; patient responsible for balance
