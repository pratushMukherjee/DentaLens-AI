# Delta Dental PPO Gold Plan

## Plan Overview
- **Plan ID:** DD-PPO-GOLD-2024
- **Network:** Delta Dental PPO
- **Annual Maximum:** $2,000 per member
- **Deductible:** $50 individual / $150 family
- **Plan Year:** January 1 – December 31

## Coverage Summary

### Preventive & Diagnostic Services (100% coverage, no deductible)
- D0120 Periodic oral evaluation: Covered 2x per calendar year
- D0150 Comprehensive oral evaluation: Covered 1x per 3 years (new patient)
- D0210 Full mouth X-rays: Covered 1x per 3 years
- D0274 Bitewing X-rays (4 films): Covered 1x per calendar year
- D1110 Adult prophylaxis (cleaning): Covered 2x per calendar year
- D1120 Child prophylaxis: Covered 2x per calendar year
- D1206 Fluoride varnish: Covered 1x per calendar year (under age 16)
- D1351 Sealants (per tooth): Covered 1x per tooth, permanent molars only (under age 16)

### Basic Services (80% after deductible)
- D2140 Silver amalgam filling (1 surface): Covered
- D2150 Silver amalgam filling (2 surfaces): Covered
- D2391 Resin-based composite (1 surface, posterior): Covered
- D2392 Resin-based composite (2 surfaces, posterior): Covered
- D3310 Root canal therapy (anterior): Covered
- D3320 Root canal therapy (premolar): Covered
- D3330 Root canal therapy (molar): Covered
- D4341 Periodontal scaling and root planing (per quadrant): Covered, 1x per quadrant per 2 years
- D4910 Periodontal maintenance: Covered after active periodontal therapy
- D7140 Extraction, erupted tooth or exposed root: Covered
- D7210 Extraction, surgical (erupted tooth): Covered

### Major Services (50% after deductible)
- D2740 Crown, porcelain/ceramic substrate: Covered, 1x per tooth per 5 years
- D2750 Crown, porcelain fused to high noble metal: Covered, 1x per tooth per 5 years
- D5110 Complete denture, maxillary: Covered, 1x per 5 years
- D5120 Complete denture, mandibular: Covered, 1x per 5 years
- D5213 Partial denture, maxillary (cast metal): Covered, 1x per 5 years
- D6240 Pontic, porcelain fused to high noble metal: Covered
- D6750 Crown (abutment), porcelain fused to high noble metal: Covered

### Orthodontic Services (50%, lifetime maximum $1,500)
- D8080 Comprehensive orthodontic treatment (adolescent): Covered
- D8090 Comprehensive orthodontic treatment (adult): Covered
- Orthodontic coverage is subject to a separate $1,500 lifetime maximum

## Waiting Periods
- Preventive & Diagnostic: None
- Basic Services: None
- Major Services: 12 months from plan effective date
- Orthodontic Services: 12 months from plan effective date

## Exclusions
- Cosmetic procedures (e.g., veneers for aesthetic purposes only)
- Dental implants (unless Major Services rider is added)
- Services covered under medical insurance
- Procedures started before the plan effective date
- Experimental or investigational treatments
- Replacement of lost or stolen prosthetic devices within 5 years

## In-Network vs Out-of-Network
- **In-network PPO dentists:** No balance billing; you pay only your share
- **Out-of-network dentists:** Covered at a reduced fee schedule; you may be responsible for the difference between the dentist's charge and the allowed amount

## How to File a Claim
1. In-network dentists file claims directly on your behalf
2. Out-of-network: Submit a completed claim form to Delta Dental within 12 months of service
3. Claims are typically processed within 10-15 business days
