# Delta Dental HMO Basic Plan

## Plan Overview
- **Plan ID:** DD-HMO-BASIC-2024
- **Network:** Delta Dental HMO (assigned primary care dentist)
- **Annual Maximum:** No annual maximum
- **Deductible:** None
- **Copay-based plan:** Fixed copays per procedure
- **Plan Year:** January 1 – December 31

## How This Plan Works
Unlike PPO plans, the HMO plan requires you to select a primary care dentist from the Delta Dental HMO network. All services must be provided by your assigned dentist or referred by them.

## Copay Schedule

### Preventive & Diagnostic Services ($0 copay)
- D0120 Periodic oral evaluation: $0 copay, 2x per year
- D0210 Full mouth X-rays: $0 copay, 1x per 3 years
- D0274 Bitewing X-rays: $0 copay, 1x per year
- D1110 Adult prophylaxis (cleaning): $0 copay, 2x per year
- D1120 Child prophylaxis: $0 copay, 2x per year

### Basic Services (fixed copays)
- D2140 Amalgam filling (1 surface): $25 copay
- D2150 Amalgam filling (2 surfaces): $35 copay
- D2391 Composite filling (1 surface, posterior): $40 copay
- D2392 Composite filling (2 surfaces, posterior): $55 copay
- D3310 Root canal (anterior): $125 copay
- D3320 Root canal (premolar): $175 copay
- D3330 Root canal (molar): $250 copay
- D4341 Periodontal scaling (per quadrant): $75 copay
- D7140 Extraction, erupted tooth: $25 copay

### Major Services (fixed copays)
- D2740 Crown, porcelain/ceramic: $300 copay (1x per tooth per 5 years)
- D2750 Crown, porcelain fused to metal: $275 copay
- D5110 Complete denture, maxillary: $350 copay
- D5120 Complete denture, mandibular: $350 copay

### Orthodontic Services
- D8080 Comprehensive orthodontic (adolescent): $1,800 copay
- D8090 Comprehensive orthodontic (adult): $2,000 copay
- Payment plans available through your dentist

## Waiting Periods
- All services: None

## Key Differences from PPO Plans
- No annual maximum — beneficial for extensive treatment needs
- No deductible — costs are predictable copays
- Must use assigned primary care dentist
- Referrals required for specialists
- No out-of-network benefits

## Exclusions
- Cosmetic procedures
- Dental implants
- Services from non-network providers (except emergencies)
- Experimental treatments
