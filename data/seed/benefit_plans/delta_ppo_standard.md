# Delta Dental PPO Standard Plan

## Plan Overview
- **Plan Type:** Delta Dental PPO (Standard)
- **Network:** Delta Dental PPO network
- **Deductible:** $25
- **Flexibility:** Members can see any licensed dentist but save money by choosing a Delta Dental PPO dentist

## Coverage Levels
- **Diagnostic & Preventive Services:** 100% with PPO dentists, no deductible
- **Basic Services:** 50% after deductible
- **Major Services:** 50% after deductible
- After out-of-pocket maximum is met: 100% coverage

## Out-of-Pocket Maximums (Essential Health Benefits)
- Single coverage: $375 per benefit year
- Two or more covered persons: $750 per benefit year

## Key Features
- Low $25 deductible
- Access to Delta Dental PPO network — the nation's largest dental network
- No waiting periods for diagnostic and preventive care
- Choices and convenience — see any licensed dentist

## Small Group Plans
- Available for groups with 2 to 9 eligible or enrolled employees
- Same benefits as standard programs, sized for smaller groups
- Contact for East Michigan: smallmarketeast@deltadentalmi.com

## Pediatric Essential Health Benefits
- No annual maximum limit for pediatric EHB services
- Oral exams covered twice yearly
- Cleanings covered three times yearly
- No waiting periods or deductibles for diagnostic and preventive care
